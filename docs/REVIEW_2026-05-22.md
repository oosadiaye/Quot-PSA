2. Flip a staging tenant to `AUTH_COOKIE_ENABLED=True`. Verify login + every protected endpoint works with the cookie. Verify logout clears the cookie.
3. Flip production tenants one wave at a time.
4. Once all tenants are on the cookie path for ≥ `TOKEN_EXPIRATION_HOURS`, remove the `localStorage` injection block from `frontend/src/api/client.ts` (~5-line cleanup) and the cookie becomes the sole auth channel.
