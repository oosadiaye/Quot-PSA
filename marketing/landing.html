The IFMIS Chain

Appropriation to payment, with controls at every step.

Quot enforces the four-stage public-sector spending lifecycle as a hard sequence—not a suggestion. You cannot commit beyond warrant. You cannot pay without commitment. You cannot post without balance. The chain is the product.

Stage 01

Appropriation

Legislative budget is enacted into NCoA segments. Original amount is snapshot for IPSAS 24 variance disclosure; evolves through virements as the Final Budget.

· Race-safe enactment · Original-amount snapshot · Virement under SoD
Stage 02

Warrant / AIE

Quarterly cash-release authority. PO and PV approvals are gated by the released-warrant ceiling, not the appropriation alone—cash discipline matches the legal authority.

· Bucketed by MDA × fund · Fail-closed on read error · MFA gate at release
Stage 03

Commitment

PO approval registers a ProcurementBudgetLink against the appropriation. Reservations move ACTIVE → INVOICED → CLOSED as goods receive and invoices verify. Encumbrance is alive.

· 3-way match · GR/IR clearing · Atomic liquidation
Stage 04

Payment & TSA

Payment Voucher posts the cash journal, debits TSA, cascades the “Paid” status to AP, IPC, and contract balance— all in one atomic transaction. Inter-TSA transfers post a balanced JV both ways.

· One-click PV → GL · Cascade-on-paid · Real-time bank balance
